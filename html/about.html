About SimpliEcon

We are a team of economists, data scientists, and storytellers dedicated to making sense of Kenya's economy.

Our Mission

To empower individuals, students, and businesses with accessible, accurate, and easy-to-understand economic data and analysis. We believe that by simplifying complex information, we can foster a more informed and engaged society.
